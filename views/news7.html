Mr. Manop Promdee has attended the Regional Training Program for Scaling up of Satellite-Assisted Flood Forecasting Systems in South and Southeast Asian Nations during 8-14 July 2017

Mr. Manop Promdee, Irrigation Engineer Practitioner Level, Water Allocation Branch 2, Water Management Division, Bureau of Water Management and Hydrology, has attended the Regional Training Program for Scaling up of Satellite-Assisted Flood Forecasting Systems in South and Southeast Asian Nations during 8-14 July 2017 in Institute of Water Modeling (IWM) Dhaka Bangladesh, to share knowledge and experience of flood prediction and management corresponding to thorough discussion with IWM staff. More information about this Training: http://www.iwmbd.org/iwmwebsite/index.php
